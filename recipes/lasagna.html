Steps to prepare

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Boil a large pot of salted water and cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  • In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  • Add chopped onions and garlic to the skillet, cook until softened.
  • Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan, and chopped parsley.
  • Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in a baking dish.
  • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  • Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  • Add a layer of mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta mixture.
  • Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  • Top the final layer with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  • Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
